Hello, I’m Alexandra Descamps…

As a native French speaker with an extroverted and charismatic personality, I am committed to optimally motivating my learners to achieve their goals. In a relaxed and positive atmosphere, my motto is: learn seriously with joy and good humor! With a master’s degree in business and experience in various roles (finance, sales, administration) across several countries, I bring extensive professional and life experience. Today, as a mother of three teenagers, I specialize in language teaching and hold the Eurolta and SVEB1 diplomas. My lessons take place at your home or in Seefeld, Zurich. Upon request, a room in Central Zurich can be rented.

I offer tailor-made programs adapted to your needs, whether for phone calls, emails, negotiations, conversation, or travel preparation. My learning method, suitable for beginners to advanced learners, supports continuous progress in general or business French. Companies, institutions, and individuals (adults, teenagers) benefit from my flexible teaching style tailored to different personalities. My strengths include targeted learning, quick and optimal goal achievement, and empathetic attention to individual needs.
